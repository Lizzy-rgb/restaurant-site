Frequently Asked Questions

Do I need an account to browse the menu?

No. You can browse the menu without signing in.

Do I need an account to place an order?

Yes. You need to sign in or register before creating an order.

I have an allergy, do I need to worry about cross-contamination?

No, we keep our kitchen partitioned; so long as you avoid menu items that have your allergy tag, you should be fine.

Are you hiring?

You're welcome to come work for us, but we're university students, so we can't afford to pay you. You have been warned.

Why doesn't my address auto-fill when I place a delivery order?

To enable this, please ensure you have "Use saved address by default" turned on in your account settings here.

It's annoying going through the modify step every time I add something to my order. Can I skip that?

Yes you can! Please enable "Skip modify order page by default" in your account settings here.

I placed an order, but I never saw my food. What happened!?

We're sorry, but that's not our not our area of expertise. Please see a psychiatrist instead.
